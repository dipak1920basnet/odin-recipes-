Instant Pot Chicken and Dumplings

Pot Chicken and Dumplings is the epitome of comfort food, a warm and hearty dish that soothes the soul with every spoonful. This quick and convenient recipe takes a classic favorite and transforms it into a weeknight dinner masterpiece. The tender pieces of chicken, combined with a medley of vegetables, create a rich and flavorful stew that's elevated by aromatic herbs and garlic. The addition of fluffy dumplings takes this dish to the next level, adding a delightful textural contrast and absorbing all the savory goodness of the stew. The magic of the Instant Pot comes into play, effortlessly infusing the flavors together in a fraction of the time it would traditionally take. It's a true time-saver without sacrificing any of the homemade goodness. As you open the lid and are met with the warm aroma of simmered chicken and herbs, it's hard not to feel a sense of home and comfort. The dumplings, fluffy and slightly crispy on top, soak up the savory broth, delivering a satisfying mouthful with each bite. Ingredients:

  • For the Chicken Stew:
    • 1.5 to 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 medium onion, chopped
    • 3 carrots, peeled and sliced
    • 3 celery stalks, sliced
    • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • 4 cups chicken broth
    • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
    • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • 1 cup frozen peas (optional)
  • For the Dumplings:
    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 tablespoon baking powder
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1 cup milk
    • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • Instructions:
    1. Set your Instant Pot to the sauté mode and add the olive oil. Once the oil is hot, add the chopped onions, carrots, and celery. Sauté for about 3-5 minutes until the vegetables start to soften.
    2. Add the minced garlic and sauté for an additional 30 seconds until fragrant.
    3. Add the chicken pieces and brown them slightly on all sides. This should take about 3-5 minutes.
    4. Pour in the chicken broth and add the dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and pepper. Give everything a good stir.
    5. Close the Instant Pot lid and set it to "Pressure Cook" on high for 10 minutes. Make sure the steam release valve is set to "Sealing."
    6. While the chicken stew is cooking, prepare the dumpling dough. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Add the melted butter and milk, and stir until a sticky dough forms.
    7. Once the pressure cooking cycle is complete, perform a quick release by turning the steam release valve to "Venting."
    8. Open the Instant Pot, and if you'd like, add the frozen peas. Stir them into the hot stew until they thaw.
    9. Spoon small portions of the dumpling dough and drop them into the hot chicken stew, making sure they're evenly distributed.
    10. Close the Instant Pot lid again and set it to "Pressure Cook" on high for an additional 5 minutes. Again, make sure the steam release valve is set to "Sealing."
    11. After the second pressure cooking cycle, perform another quick release.
    12. Carefully open the Instant Pot lid and give the chicken and dumplings a gentle stir.
    13. Serve the chicken and dumplings hot in bowls, garnished with fresh parsley if desired.
